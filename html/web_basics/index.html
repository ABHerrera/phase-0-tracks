The best hotdogs have the following basics:
(Note this is just a starting list of yummy things that make up the best hotdogs...the list is almost infinite.)
  • a high-quality all meat hotdog or sausage
  • a warm hotdog bun
  • a sauce or two
  • toppings

Topping are of particular importance. You can have the same base hotdog but totally take it in a different direction. For example, if you add kimchi and something tasty it becomes more of a Korean flared hotdog versus adding avocado to the top of your hotdog which can take it a fresh farmersmarket or Mexican flared route.

When I make a hotdog bar for friends I always have the following toppings available:
  1. jalapenos
  2. mango
  3. peaches
  4. bacon
  5. cilantro
  6. onions
  7. kimchi
  8. chips (different flavors)
  9. some sort of green like arugula or kale

I'll expand on the toppings based on the time of year but the list above is always a good foundation for a hotdog topping bar!
